About This Calculator

This calculator estimates SIP returns adjusted for Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax.

Formula: FV = P × [((1 + i)^n - 1) / i] × (1 + i)

  • P = Monthly Investment
  • i = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12 / 100)
  • n = Number of Months (Years × 12)

Note: This is a simplified model. Real-world taxation may vary based on exemptions (e.g., ₹1 Lakh annual exemption in India) and fund types.
